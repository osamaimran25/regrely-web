The problem

Fragmented spreadsheets, manual evidence gathering, and unclear ownership expose teams to SLA misses and audit risk.

  • Inconsistent DSAR handling across departments
  • No real-time view of compliance findings
  • Difficulty proving tenant isolation and RBAC controls

The RegRely solution

RegRely centralizes privacy operations and risk workflows with policy-linked controls and evidence packs.

  • Automated workflows with approval checkpoints
  • Audit trails and export-ready records per tenant
  • Risk register connected to remediation actions
