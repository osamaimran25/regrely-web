About RegRely

We built RegRely for teams priced out of enterprise compliance software

Our mission is simple: help mid-market companies prove GDPR compliance with the same confidence as enterprise teams, without paying enterprise prices or waiting half a year to go live.

Our story

RegRely started after seeing the same pattern again and again. Growing SaaS, Fintech, and Healthtech companies were being asked to show serious compliance proof by auditors, investors, and enterprise customers, but the tools available to them were built for much larger organizations.

The options were painful: keep running compliance in spreadsheets and shared drives, or buy a heavy enterprise suite that cost thousands per month and took months to configure. Teams did not need more complexity. They needed a practical system that worked fast and produced clean, defensible evidence.

So we built RegRely to be the right-sized alternative: fast onboarding, clear workflows, and transparent pricing. We are here for teams that want to do compliance properly, without turning implementation into a second full-time job.

What we value

Clarity over complexity

Compliance work is hard enough. The software should make priorities obvious, not buried in settings.

Proof over promises

Every workflow should end in usable evidence you can share with auditors, boards, and customers.

Speed with accountability

Fast onboarding matters, but only if ownership, due dates, and outcomes are clear across teams.

Fair pricing for real teams

Mid-market teams should not be forced into enterprise contracts just to run a serious privacy program.
